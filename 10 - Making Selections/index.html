Making Selections

This is an inbox layout.

It's a fairly common layout

Especially in mail clients

Check one item

Hold down your Shift key

Check a lower item

Or a higher item

Everything inbetween will be checked

Done without any libraries

Just regular JavaScript

Seems simple enough!

This is accomplished

By looping through the list

After setting a flag variable

And checking everything

Between the flags!
